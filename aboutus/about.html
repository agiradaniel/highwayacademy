About Us

Our History

When he retired, Mr Ali had ventured into poultry farming,But the business was not doing well owing to the high cost of feeds and scarcity of clean water for the birds. Hotels that consumed his broilers were never willing to make prompt payments

"Eventually I decided to do away with poultry business but the structures remained intact.One day, a friend to my wife challenged us to modify the buildings into classrooms to tap on the increasing number of school going children in the neighbourhood," explains Mr Ali

After giving this advice a thought, Highway Academy Kokotoni was born in 2008 with the first classroom catering for Early Child Development(ECD). Since then, it has become a norm to put up a classroom every year as the pupils go up the education ladder.

Our Mission

To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of the society

Our Vision

To empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school.

Our Values

Cooperation: Sharing and learning together

Achievement: Striving to attain your goals, persistence and giving your best effort

Respect: Taking responsibility for each other and the environment, being honest, reliable and trustworthy

Empathy: Understanding other people's views and being supportive and caring towards them.
